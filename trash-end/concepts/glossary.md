# Working glossary

These definitions are deliberately narrow. They keep adjacent concepts connected without making them interchangeable.

## Aisthesis

Perception, sensation, or the capacity for feeling. In this tree it names the recovery of the biological body as interface and medium of experience, not merely visual beauty.

## Anthropocentrism

The habit of treating human form, intelligence, language, value, or purpose as the measure or center of reality.

## Autopoiesis

The continuing production and maintenance of the organization and boundary that constitute a living system. It is not proved by self-charging, self-repair, or replication alone.

## Biological digital twin

A proposed digital representation of a living counterpart with persistent identity, updating, dynamic modeling, and some monitoring, simulation, or predictive relation to the organism. A static scan is not enough.

## Computation

A physical or formal process that transforms information according to constraints or rules. This archive does not assume that every material change is computation in the same meaningful sense.

## Computronium

A limiting idea of matter organized so that physical resources are used as efficiently as possible for computation. Here it also names a speculative future-facing work, not a claim that the limit has been reached.

## Consciousness

Subjective experience, whose conditions and possible substrates remain open questions. Intelligence, language, prediction, and computation do not individually prove it.

## First skin

The biological body as the first boundary, filter, and interface through which the project encounters the world. Trash-End initially resists it; Aisthesis recovers it; Synthesis studies its transformations.

## Information

Organization or difference that can be represented, constrained, transmitted, or acted upon. Information is not automatically meaning, life, computation, or consciousness.

## Pattern

A recurring relation or organization. A detected pattern may be real, projected, or useful despite remaining unproved. The tree treats pattern recognition as both method and risk.

## Proto-computronic

A direction in which increasing amounts of matter, energy, and infrastructure are deliberately organized around computation, while remaining far from the physical limiting idea of computronium.

## Synthesis

The reconciliation layer that studies transformations between biology and machine, matter and information, roots and future. It is not evidence that these fields already share one formal theory.
