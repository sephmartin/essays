# Aisthesis

**Status:** emerging work
**Current material:** two Italian conceptual seeds

Aisthesis is the return to the first biological skin after the original attempt to transcend it. The body returns without becoming the center of the universe: it is interface, memory, evolutionary archive, and the medium through which patterns become felt experience.

## Index

1. [Quando l'informazione vibra](music.md) — music as vibration, rhythm, bodily synchronization, expectation, memory, emotion, and social form.
2. [Attrazione: un'estetica del sentire](attraction.md) — attraction as multisensory orientation that can precede verbal explanation.
3. [Rhythm across life: sources and boundaries](sources/README.md) — animal entrainment, collective timing, plant vibration responses, and the limits of the analogy.
4. [Synthesis](../synthesis/README.md) — the bridge that follows the recovery of the body.

## Working questions

- Why does information, when it vibrates, become emotion?
- At which biological scales can a temporal pattern coordinate a response without becoming music or felt groove?
- What does the body recognize before the mind justifies it?
- Which parts of perception are evolved, learned, embodied, cultural, or relational?
- What would be preserved or lost if intelligence moved to another substrate?

The seeds do not claim that music is only information, that attraction follows one universal formula, or that bodily recognition is infallible.
